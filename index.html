Mana Manipulation
The most basic class of magic, involving the direct manipulation of Mana, and is available to all mages. Raw Mana is a mundane source of magic with no special qualities, but offers some level of versatility. The amount of Mana a mage is capable of replenishing or expelling is dependent on their overall level.
What are Universal Spells?
Universal spells are spells taught by the teachers of Ulreich University that any mage can learn no matter their tree so long as their current rank is high enough to learn it.
Personalized Mana
When mana is being weaved throughout Tanya's body, it takes on the form a medium toned purple flame that coils off from her skin like a fine mist and with no heat, ribbons of light blue intermixing with the purple like an intricate design. This is more prominent when spells like Show of Power are being used, and on its own it isn't too distracting, the colors more transparent than opaque. However, this aura becomes more opaque when more mana is being used, causing large-scale spells to cover her entire body, making her a beacon of purple and blue heat-less flames.

Enchantment


Strong like Bear The caster enchants a non-magical item no bigger than can be comfortably be held in one hand to bolster the holder's physical strength by 1.5x and may only be in effect so long as the object remains in physical contact with the caster. Leaving contact will cause the enchantment to cancel instantly and the spell also drains mana continuously until canceled. This enchantment may last for a maximum duration of two posts before automatically dispelling and going on a one post cool down duration.

Russian Winds
When being moved through the air, the enchanted object ( which can not be too big to be held in one hand ) will begin to manipulate the air around itself to build up energy. Once enough energy has been built up over the course of two posts, the object will release an omnidirectional blast of cold wind which not only can knock back an average strength human a few feet, but is cold enough to cause mild chills and make wet objects almost freezing cold. After being enchanted and the item's ability is used, this enchantment fades away and goes on a two post cool down time before being able to be cast again on any object.

"Davka"
A spell that has been affectionately named "Crush" when translated into the common language, this enchantment allows small handheld items to be thrown as projectiles and exponentially gain weight as they go. This allows for a greater punch when being hit, but certainly can not pierce through higher-grade materials than the object itself is. Objects with Davka cast on them gain an additional pound for every one foot they travel, up to a maximum of an extra fifteen pounds by traveling fifteen feet. Once the object leaves this fifteen foot area, the enchantment is instantly dispelled and the object loses all weight gained from being thrown.
